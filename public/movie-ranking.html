Matt's Favorite Movies

My top movies of all time.

The Lord of the Rings trilogy

These films are by no means perfect, and I disagree with some of the changes from the novels. But Peter Jackson redefined what is considered possible in films with these; I don't think something with this scope will be produced again in my lifetime.

Hedwig and the Angry Inch

This film introduced me to new ways of being, and redefined what it is to be queer. The musical is also one of my favorite of all time.

The Devil Wears Prada

Meryl Streep is sublime as Miranda Priestly. I want to be her.

Death Becomes Her

So camp. So much fun. Another favorite of mine for Meryl Streep.
